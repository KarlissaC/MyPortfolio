About Me

Where AI Meets Business Strategy

I'm an AI-forward Product Manager who bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and real business outcomes. With experience spanning healthcare, hospitality, legal, and finance — I build products that actually move the needle. I love cooking and jogging. Let’s be honest: nobody wants to use an AI product that feels like a fragile science experiment. 🧪

In my world as an AI Product Manager, the magic doesn't happen when you simply slap an LLM onto a legacy dashboard. It happens when you stop building for the tech and start building for the human on the other side of the screen.

I build AI products the way I run a kitchen: I figure out what people are actually hungry for, taste everything before it leaves the pass, and never serve a dish just because the recipe was trending on TikTok.

My sweet spot? Translating bleeding-edge capabilities like Generative AI, machine learning, and complex automated workflows—into intuitive, high-value user experiences. To pull this off, I rely on a dual approach: architecting robust technical foundations (so the product scales without hallucinating under load) and leveraging deep behavioral insights (because psychology is what actually drives adoption).

When you balance the technical "how it works" with the psychological "why they need it," you stop merely launching features. You accelerate real business growth, optimize market positioning, and build AI tools that your users genuinely trust.

I'm also a lifelong learner — certified across AI, ML, data analysis, and product strategy through Harvard, Vanderbilt, Duke, Google, NVIDIA, and more.
